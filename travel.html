Visit Paris

Paris, located in France, is one of the most influential cities in the world. It is often recognized as the "City of Love." Apart from that, Paris is home to a lot of historical sites.

Why you want to visit Paris

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Seine River
  • Notre Dame Church
  • Napoleon's Tomb
  • Sacre Coeur

Why you want to visit the Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel tower is one of the major touristic places in the world. It is 300 meters tall, and 324 meters tall, to the tip.

Why you want to visit the Seine River

  1. It is 777 kilometers long.
  2. It has been used for artery and transportation since Roman Times.
  3. It is an iconic part of Paris.

Why to visit Norte Dame

Norte Dame is a very popular church, and one of the most recognized in the world. It has the biggest organ in France, having over 8000 pipes. The Cathedral has a gothic city lying below it. Over 28 kings were believed to lose their heads there.

Why you want to visit Napoleon's Tomb

Napoleon's tomb is a place very often visited in France. The French, Emperor, Napoleon Bonaparte, was buried here. It is made in a way that you need to kneel to see it, he requested that.

Why you want to visit the Sacre Coeur

The Sacre Coeur is a basilica in Paris, France. It is dedicated to the Sacred Heart of Jesus.
